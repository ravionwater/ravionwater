Purified Water Storage Distribution

The storage and distribution network is a critical component of any water purification facility. After treatment, purified water is stored and continuously recirculated throughout the distribution loop until it reaches the designated points of use. Our storage and distribution solutions are engineered to preserve water quality throughout the system, ensuring that key purity parameters remain stable without deterioration during storage and circulation.

PW, WFI Loop Piping

Design of a good distribution system for critical fluids like Purified Water, Water for Injection, Clean steam, Sterile air, Nitrogen etc., is very critical to deliver the high quality of fluid generated at the generation source. The fluid has to be distributed to the user points, long distances, with nil or minimal degradation of quality. The key factors that affect the distribution system are; • Design • Material of construction • Surface finish (SF4) • Installation • Drainability • Dead leg elimination
